MULTIPLE DISABILITIES

Multiple disabilities is a term for a person with a combination of disabilities.
for instance, someone with both a sensory disability and a motor disability.

Types of Multiple disabilities:


Multiple Disabilities including Deaf Blindness

Deafblindness is a unique disability that combines varying degrees of both hearing and visual impairment
All individuals who are deafblind experience extreme challenges with communication.
The way they access information, and mobility and most have additional physical and medical conditions.
